About Me

Hello! I'm Caprica, a passionate hairstylist dedicated to helping everyone I work with to express their true self through their hair.

With a specialty in gender-affirming haircuts, I strive to create styles that align with your identity and make you feel confident and authentic.

I also excel in low-maintenance, lived-in hair painting allowing you to go longer between your color appointments and still look effortlessly chic.

Additionally, my love for vibrant colors means I can transform your look with stunning, vivid hues that showcase your personality.

My goal is to make every client of mine feel like their favorite version of themselves, whether that's through a subtle change or a bold transformation. Book a consultation now and let's work together to create a look that you love and feel great in every day!
